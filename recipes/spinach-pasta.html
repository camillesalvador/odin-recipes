Creamy spinach pasta with caramelized shallots

"The answer to a perfect creamy pasta doesn't always have to be heavy cream. In this new recipe, we will show you have to make a plate of creamy pasta with a secret ingredient: pasta water. Topped with caramelized shallots and pistachios, it will surprise you with how easy and delicious it can be!"

Ingredients

  • 250 g rigatoni
  • 150 g baby spinach
  • 2 shallots
  • 40 g pistachios
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 150 ml pasta water
  • 60 g butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and season with salt. Meanwhile, wash the lemon under hot water and zest it. Halve the shallots and cut them into fine strips. Finely chop garlic.
  2. Add pasta to boiling water and cook according to package instructions. At the end of the pasta cooking time, reserve some cooking water. Meanwhile, continue with the recipe. In a large frying pan, melt a third of the butter. Add pistachios, shallot strips, and sugar and sauté until butter is browned and shallots are lightly caramelized for approx. 4 - 6 min. Remove from pan and set aside in a small bowl.
  3. Heat more oil in the same frying pan. Add garlic and sauté for 1 min. until translucent. Add cooked pasta and deglaze everything with reserved pasta cooking water. Bring to a boil and add the remaining butter. Reserve some grated Parmesan cheese for garnish, and add most of it to the pasta. Mix everything thoroughly. Continue cooking and stirring until the pasta is coated with sauce and becomes glossy, for about 4 min.
  4. Add baby spinach to the pasta, mix, and let it wilt. Season pasta with a little salt and lemon zest. Then season generously with pepper. Arrange pasta on deep plates, top with pistachios and shallots, and garnish with remaining lemon zest and parmesan. Serve immediately.
